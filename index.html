My Project

In our Year 10 Digital Technology class, we built games for people to play at the Year 10 Market Day. My project was Balloon pop

Balloon Pop is a game where you have darts and you balloons and you pay to try pop the balloons. There is a mirco bit that when it heres a pop noice it lit up on the micro bit. there is a person on the sides spinning a peice of rope to make the balloons move around so it wasnt to easy to pop the balloons.

Balloon Pop had some problems along the way of the process trying to make it. One of the problems we have was how to get the balloon going around without it falling of or getting stuck when it goes around the corner. We stopped this problem by making sure there was more room for the balloon when it went around the corner by using an extender.

For the coding for Balloon Pop we used a microbit. The microbit picked up the sound of the balloon popping. It sended a radio signal to another micobit that Eva was holding. The microbit would only pick up sound if it was above the sound level 70. This wasnt a easy task if the sound level was to low it would pick up everything but if it was to high it wouldnt pick up anything. To make sure we had theperfect sound level we would clap as loud as a balloon would pop next to the microbit.

For the building side of Balloon Pop we used an EPro8 kit to build the base of our game. We all had a seperate jobs Eva was the builder and Annabel was the ideas girl and I was the designer that designed all the logos and prices. Once we had all the building part was over and we had printed out all our logos we sent it down to the office to get it laminated. Then we stuck it on the Balloon Pop base. Annabel and I was went down to the gym to go find a dart board to hope that there is darts in it there none. We asked Mr Hullen to get us some darts and balloons and he did. While we waited for them we thought we need something to be at the back of the target so the dart sticks. Our group went down to the skip and found some old card board. We trimmed it down to the size we needed it to be. Once the darts and balloons arrived we blew up some balloons and stuck it on to Balloon Pop string it keeped falling of Annabel decided to put on rubber bands before stuck the balloons on so we could tie them on to the rubber bands. It ended up working very good.
